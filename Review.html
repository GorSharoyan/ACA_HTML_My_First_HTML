The HPE Synergy 12000 Frame is the base for an HPE Synergy intelligent infrastructure with embedded management and scalable links that expands to meet business demand. The Frame is the base infrastructure that pools resources of compute, storage, fabric, cooling, power and scalability. IT can manage, assemble and scale resources on demand by using the Synergy Frame with an embedded management solution combining the Synergy Composer and Frame Link Modules. The Synergy Frame is designed to meet today’s needs and future needs with continuing enhancements to compute and fabric bandwidths, including photonics-ready capabilities.
